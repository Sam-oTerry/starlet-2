  • User Roles: Buyers, sellers, agents (with tiered subscriptions: Silver, Gold, Diamond, where Diamond offers priority visibility), staff, and administrators.

Our mission is to provide a seamless, transparent, and secure platform for property and vehicle transactions in Uganda.
