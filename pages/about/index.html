Starlet Properties

Uganda's premier real estate and vehicle marketplace. We connect buyers, sellers, and trusted agents across the country, making property and vehicle transactions simple, transparent, and secure.

Our Mission

To empower Ugandans with a modern, reliable, and user-friendly platform for buying, selling, and renting properties and vehicles. We strive to build trust, promote transparency, and support economic growth by connecting people with the best agents, official stores, and verified listings nationwide.

Our Story

Founded by a passionate team of real estate and technology professionals, Starlet Properties was created to address the challenges faced by buyers and sellers in Uganda's property and vehicle markets. Our platform leverages the latest technology and local expertise to deliver a seamless experience for all users, whether you're searching for your dream home, a new car, or a trusted agent to guide you.

Trust & Verification

All official stores and agents are verified for your peace of mind. Look for the verification badge on listings and stores.

Official Stores

Partner with Uganda's top real estate and vehicle stores for the best deals and service.

Agent Tiers

Choose from a range of agent tiers, from independent agents to official partners, to suit your needs and budget.

Localization

Our platform is tailored for Uganda, with local language support, regional filters, and up-to-date market data.

Diverse Listings

Find properties and vehicles of all types, from houses and land to cars, motorcycles, and more.
